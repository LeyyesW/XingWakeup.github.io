Wake-up

醒

娱乐泛滥的时代里，电视创造出的没有连续性、没有意义的世界，经由互联网进一步加深，于是我们越来越适应没有连贯性的世界，被娱乐得麻木不仁，不再对世间的痛苦有所共情，同情心也被深深掩埋。
最终通过不断的试验与调试完成该媒介交互装置，并将其命名为————「醒」，意在通过对观众交互后的结果，而得出的社会感知力报告来唤醒人们心中对于社会的感知。装置外表也酷似一个刑具，让其更加富有审判的寓意在其中，通过这一种变相且轻微的“行刑”方式来唤醒现如今人们逐渐淡化的社会感知力。

The world of discontinuity and meaninglessness created by television in an era of overflowing entertainment has been reinforced by the Internet, and we have become more and more accustomed to a world of discontinuity, oblivious to entertainment, empathetic to the suffering of the world, and deeply buried in compassion.
Finally, this media interaction device was completed through continuous experiments and debugging, and named ———— "Wake-up", aiming to awaken people's perception of society through the social perception report obtained from the interaction results of the audience. The appearance of the device is also similar to a torture device, which makes it more full of the implication of trial. Through this disguised and slight "execution" way, it awaken people's gradually weakened social perception nowadays.
